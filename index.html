About Me

I'm an Electronics & Instrumentation Engineer driven by the vision of merging hardware intelligence with cutting-edge software to solve real-world problems.

My journey spans across IoT & embedded systems, industrial automation, and biomedical signal processing. I've had the privilege of interning at India's top research institutions — IIT Bhubaneswar, NIT Warangal, and IISc Bangalore — where I designed smart automation prototypes, programmed industrial PLCs, and explored brain-computer interfaces.

Currently, I'm building an fNIRS analysis pipeline that extracts hemodynamic brain signals from a consumer-grade Muse S headband, applying the Modified Beer-Lambert Law, wavelet denoising, and advanced artifact correction — making neuroscience research more accessible.
